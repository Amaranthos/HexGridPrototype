Eir, the legendary valkyrie watches over her maidens and tends to their wounds. With her blessing, Eir's Maiden works to keep all of her troops in perfect health, despite their opposition.;
Heimdall, the immovable guardian of the Bifrost. Those who follow him take after his steadfast nature, boasting more powerful defensive formations and capabilities. Heimdall's Guardian is also able to make use of the Bifrost itself, for immediate traversal of the battlefield.;
Skadi, the Winter Goddess and master of bow and spear. Following her teachings, Skadi's Huntress makes the most of her spearmen in every battle. Additionally, years in the mountains has fortified her army to be unhindered by the frigid tundra.;
Thor, God of Thunder and stalwart warrior of Asgard. He is the ideal warrior mortal men aspire to be. Thor's Warrior inspires the troops around him, boosting their courage and offense.;
Sam, the legendary god of metal himself. He refuses to allow any other to represent him on the battlefield, lest they take all the fun. As the greatest of all the gods, his power is unimaginable. He will not be defeated in combat.
